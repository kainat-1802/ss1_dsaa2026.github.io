IEEE DSAA 2026 Special Session

DS4SG: Data Science for Social Good

Welcome to the Special Session on Data Science for Social Good (DS4SG) at IEEE DSAA 2026. This session aims to bring together researchers and practitioners working on impactful AI and data-driven solutions addressing real-world societal challenges.

📢 Call for Papers

We invite high-quality submissions on innovative research in data science for social good, including but not limited to:

  • AI for healthcare
  • Climate and sustainability
  • Education and accessibility
  • Ethical AI and fairness
  • Public policy and governance

📅 Important Dates

  • Paper Submission: May 30, 2026
  • Notification: August 10, 2026
  • Camera Ready: August 30, 2026
  • Conference Dates: October 6–9, 2026

📄 Submission Guidelines

Submissions should follow IEEE DSAA formatting guidelines and be submitted via the official conference system.
