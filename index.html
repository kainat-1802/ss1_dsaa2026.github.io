IEEE DSAA 2026 Special Session

Advancing Data Science for Real-World Impact

🌍 About the Session

The DS4SG special session provides a multidisciplinary platform for advancing data science methodologies and applications that contribute to societal good. It bridges foundational research with real-world impact by promoting ethical, transparent, and human-centered AI systems.

The session emphasizes solutions that improve quality of life, empower underserved communities, and support sustainable development. We encourage work that demonstrates measurable impact, tackles real-world constraints, and critically examines risks and limitations of data-driven systems.

📢 Call for Papers

We invite high-quality original submissions on data science and AI solutions addressing societal challenges across domains such as healthcare, climate, education, and public policy.

🔍 Topics of Interest

  • Healthcare analytics & public health
  • Climate change & environmental data science
  • Learning analytics & education systems
  • Fairness, ethics, and responsible AI
  • Social welfare, inequality, and inclusion
  • Public policy & civic engagement
  • Sustainability & energy systems
  • Agriculture & food security
  • Digital humanities & cultural analytics
  • Interpretability & trustworthy AI
  • Datasets, benchmarks, tools & real-world case studies

📄 Submission Guidelines

  • Original, unpublished work (no dual submissions)
  • Double-blind review (no author details in paper)
  • Max 7 pages + references (IEEE format)
  • PDF submission following DSAA guidelines
  • Optional supplementary material allowed
  • Reproducibility encouraged (code/data sharing)
  • COI must be declared during submission
  • At least one author must present accepted papers

Note: Non-compliant submissions (format/anonymity) will be rejected without review.

📅 Important Dates

  • Notification: August 10, 2026
  • Camera Ready: August 30, 2026
  • Conference: October 6–9, 2026
